Creator Partnerships

Co-developing the next generation of spiritual wellness experiences with the creators who shape the conversation.

The Program

Celestial Ventures partners with select creators in the astrology, spirituality, and wellness space to co-develop branded digital products and experiences for their audiences. Our creator partners leverage our technology infrastructure, product development capabilities, and operational scale to bring their vision to life — without the complexity of building from scratch.

What We Provide

Technology & Product

We provide the full technology stack — from app development and infrastructure to ongoing product iteration. Our platforms are built for scale, serving millions of daily active users across our portfolio.

Revenue & Monetization

Our creator partners earn recurring revenue through revenue-share models designed for long-term growth. We handle billing, subscription management, and customer support — creators focus on what they do best.

Brand & Distribution

We bring cross-portfolio distribution, brand development resources, and access to our network of wellness and spiritual content platforms. Our partnerships are long-term — we invest in the relationship, not just the transaction.

Who We Work With

We partner with creators who have built authentic, engaged audiences in the spiritual wellness space — astrology, tarot, meditation, energy healing, mindfulness, and adjacent categories. Our ideal partners are established voices with 100K+ followers who are ready to expand beyond content into branded digital products.
