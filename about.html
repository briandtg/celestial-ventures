About Us

Our Mission & Approach

Building the infrastructure behind the world’s leading spiritual wellness brands since 2016.

Our Mission

Celestial Ventures was founded in 2016 with a singular thesis: that the intersection of technology and spiritual wellness would produce the next generation of enduring consumer brands. A decade later, that thesis has been validated by a portfolio spanning 24 companies across meditation, astrology, mindfulness, and spiritual content — platforms that collectively serve over 180 million users monthly.

Our Approach

We operate at the intersection of three disciplines: consumer technology, spiritual wellness, and digital media. We build, acquire, and invest across the full lifecycle — from seed-stage platforms with breakout potential to established category leaders seeking strategic capital and operational partnership.

Our investment philosophy centers on platforms with deep user engagement, recurring usage patterns, and authentic connections to the communities they serve. We believe the spiritual wellness category is defined by trust — users return to the products and creators they trust — and our portfolio strategy reflects that conviction.

Our Team

Our team brings decades of combined experience across consumer technology, venture capital, wellness, and digital media. We draw on operating experience at leading technology firms, media companies, and investment platforms — a breadth of perspective that informs every investment thesis and operational decision.

We are headquartered in New York with offices in London and Singapore, reflecting the global scale of the markets we serve.

The Opportunity

The global wellness market represents a $5.6 trillion opportunity. Within that, spiritual wellness — astrology, meditation, mindfulness, energy healing — is one of the fastest-growing segments, driven by a generational shift toward personalized, technology-enabled spiritual experiences. Celestial Ventures is positioned at the center of this transformation.

24 portfolio companies. Over 180 million monthly users. More than $2.1 billion in capital deployed across 40+ countries.
